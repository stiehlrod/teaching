# Teaching Resources

A collection of physical education teaching materials for elementary and secondary levels.

## Structure

```
teaching/
├── elementary/
│   ├── Assessment/          # Rubrics and assessment tools
│   ├── Classroom-Exercises/ # Brain gym and energizer activities
│   ├── Housekeeping/        # Rules, protocols, and behavior management
│   ├── Preschool/           # Early childhood PE resources
│   ├── Resources/           # General teaching resources
│   ├── Teaching-Tools/      # Planning tools, research, and student materials
│   └── Yearly-Plans/
│       └── Units/
│           ├── By-Grade/           # Grade-specific lesson plans (K, 3rd)
│           ├── Fitness-&-Wellness/ # Anatomy, fitness, nutrition
│           ├── Games_Sports/       # Net games, street games, team sports
│           ├── Gymnastics/         # Tumbling and gymnastics
│           ├── Rhythms/            # Dance and rhythm activities
│           ├── Skills/             # Dribbling, jumping, throwing
│           └── Social-&-Personal/  # Teambuilding and cooperation
│
└── secondary/
    ├── Methods_Sec_PE/      # Secondary PE methods and basketball unit
    ├── Multicultural/       # Multicultural education resources
    ├── Rdg3280/             # Reading course materials
    ├── S_M_T_Weight_Training/ # Weight training curriculum
    └── Swearingen/          # Class photos and PE components
```

## Contents

### Elementary
- **Assessment**: Sample rubrics for PE skills evaluation
- **Classroom Exercises**: Brain gym activities and K-5 energizers for classroom-based physical activity
- **Yearly Plans**: Comprehensive unit plans covering fitness, sports, gymnastics, rhythms, and social skills
- **Teambuilding**: Cooperative games, trust activities, and risk management forms

### Secondary
- **Methods**: Secondary PE teaching methods with complete basketball unit (20 lesson plans)
- **Weight Training**: Resistance training and stretching curriculum
- **Swearingen**: Swimming, diving, archery, and various PE activity photos

## File Formats

All documents have been converted to PDF for universal accessibility. Original Office files (.doc, .xls, .xlsx) were converted using LibreOffice.
